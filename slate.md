Cyber Risk and Compliance: Building Resilience Through AI, Data Governance, and Cross-Team Collaboration

Cybersecurity is no longer just an IT issue—it’s a top-tier compliance risk. As threats evolve and regulatory expectations tighten, CCOs must play a central role in shaping their firm’s cybersecurity and data governance strategy. This session explores how compliance leaders are partnering with cyber, privacy, and IT teams to build resilient programs that address breach response, privacy laws, and governance frameworks. We’ll also examine how artificial intelligence is transforming both the threat landscape and the tools available to defend against it—highlighting how AI can enhance threat detection, automate governance processes, and support cross-functional collaboration in a rapidly changing regulatory environment.

Beyond the Deal: Strengthening Portfolio Oversight in ESG and Cybersecurity

As regulatory expectations and stakeholder demands intensify, CCO in private equity face growing pressure to ensure robust oversight across portfolio companies—especially in the critical areas of ESG and cybersecurity. Join industry experts and peers for a deep dive into how CCOs can lead the charge in embedding ESG and cyber resilience into the DNA of their portfolios—without compromising deal velocity or value creation.
